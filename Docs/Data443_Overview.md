# Data443 – Core Overview & Technical Documentation

*(Extracted and rewritten from 2025-Data443-basic info.pdf)*

## 1. Introduction to Data443

Data443 is a cybersecurity and data governance company focused on identifying, protecting, managing, and recovering sensitive data across all environments.
Its mission is described as “organizing the world’s information by identifying & protecting sensitive data regardless of location, platform, or format.”

## 2. Company Timeline & Key Milestones (2018–2025)

The timeline outlines major acquisitions, product releases, partnerships, and certifications that shaped Data443’s evolution. Key highlights:

*   **2018:** Acquisition of ClassiDocs; WordPress GDPR plugin global rights; partnership with Citrix.

## 4. Data443 Technology Stack

A wide technology stack covering the entire lifecycle of data security:

*   **Data Classification:** Automated tagging & classification of structured and unstructured data—at rest or in motion.
*   **Data Security:** Cloud-based protection and workflow automation; governance; secure board portals.
*   **Data Archiving:** Enterprise archiving, e-discovery support, and long-term compliance.
*   **Data Identification:** Rapid identification needed for privacy laws (GDPR, CPRA, etc.).
*   **Data Control:** Fine-grained access control for cloud platforms like Box, Google Workspace, and OneDrive.
*   **Data Transfer (MFT):** Secure exchange of sensitive data across organizations.
*   **Data Knowledge:** Automated document-type recognition.
*   **Device Recovery:** Endpoint-level recovery after ransomware—“simple reboot under any condition.”
*   **Device Protection:** Email security, spam detection, malware protection, URL filtering, threat intelligence.
*   **Inbox Management (Private Email Platform):**
    *   No provider oversight
    *   AI-powered inbox sorting
    *   Advanced threat detection

## 6. Product Portfolio (2025)

*   **Data Placement Manager:** Secure transfer between organizations.
*   **Ransomware Recovery Manager:** Full system recovery for PCs, servers, and cloud instances.
*   **Sensitive Content Manager:** Distribute confidential data without leakage.
